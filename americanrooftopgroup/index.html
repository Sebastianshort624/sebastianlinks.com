The Business Model
🏠

In-Home Estimators

Each rep visits homeowners, assesses roof condition, and presents ARG's broker pricing — showing real savings vs. standard contractor quotes on the spot.

🤝

Broker Contracts

ARG signs exclusive territory agreements with 2–3 top roofing companies per market. They honor 30–50% below retail in exchange for a guaranteed, steady pipeline of sold jobs.

💰

ARG Margin

15–25%

ARG marks up above broker cost but remains far below retail. Homeowner wins. Roofer wins. ARG keeps the spread.

📍

1 Location / Month

Systematic rollout — new metro area activated each month. Each location is self-sustaining within 60–90 days.

🎯

Commission-Only Reps

No salary burden. Reps earn cash per bid converted. Top reps earn $3,000–$6,000/week in active markets.

📈

Scalable Stack

Each market replicates the same playbook: broker deal → lead funnels → estimator → close. Overhead stays lean. Profit compounds.

How It Works — Step by Step

Chris negotiates a written contract with 2–3 licensed roofers per market guaranteeing 30–50% off retail per job delivered.

2

Paid digital ads, direct mail, and storm-response canvassing generate homeowner leads at $30–$80 per qualified request.

3

Inbound leads are filtered by a call center or VA, then booked for an in-home estimate within 48 hours.

ARG estimator visits, assesses roof, presents broker pricing. Homeowner sees 30–50% savings vs. getting their own quotes.

Rep closes the deal on the spot. Deposit collected. Rep earns cash commission same day. ARG sends the job to broker roofer.

6

Broker roofer installs the roof. Homeowner pays balance to ARG. ARG pays roofer at broker rate. ARG keeps the margin.

Broker Contract Strategy

What ARG Offers Roofing Companies

  • A guaranteed pipeline of pre-sold jobs — no marketing cost to them
  • Exclusive territory rights within the ARG market area
  • Homeowner has already committed — roofer just shows up and installs
  • Consistent project volume month over month
  • No chasing invoices — ARG collects and pays on schedule
  • First right of refusal on premium upsell add-ons (gutters, insulation, skylight)

What ARG Requires From Roofing Companies

  • Written contract with fixed broker pricing (30–50% below ARG's retail quote)
  • Active license, insurance, and bonding in the state
  • 3-star minimum Google rating with verifiable reviews
  • Written workmanship warranty (minimum 5 years)
  • Capacity to handle minimum 8–12 jobs per month per market
  • Commitment to ARG brand standards during installation visits
  • Priority scheduling for ARG clients (48–72 hr project start)
