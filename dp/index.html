FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my move?
For local moves, 2–3 weeks is ideal. For long-distance or peak-season summer moves, book 4–6 weeks in advance to lock in your preferred date.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. DP Movers Group LLC is fully licensed under USDOT regulations and carries full cargo and liability insurance on every move, every time.
How is my quote calculated?
Quotes are based on inventory size, distance, access, and services required. We provide written, binding estimates — no surprise charges on moving day.
Do you provide packing supplies?
Yes. We provide all packing materials including boxes, tape, bubble wrap, dish-pack inserts, wardrobe boxes, and custom crating for specialty items.
What if something gets damaged?
All moves include full-value protection. In the unlikely event of damage, we resolve it directly — no third-party claims process, no runaround.
Do you charge for stairs or long carries?
We're transparent about all charges upfront. Any applicable fees for stairs, long carries, or elevator waits are discussed and agreed upon before your move date.
