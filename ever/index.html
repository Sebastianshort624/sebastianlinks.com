💡 Hiding Fiber — She Won't Notice
Blend carrots, zucchini, or spinach into pasta sauce
Add 1 tbsp chia seeds to oatmeal, yogurt, or smoothies — undetectable
Stir ground flaxseed into anything — no taste, no texture
Use whole wheat pasta — tastes nearly the same
Blend lentils into taco meat or chili — invisible
Blend black beans into burger patties or meatballs
Purée cauliflower into mac and cheese sauce
Finely chop bell peppers into scrambled eggs
📈
Increase fiber gradually over 1–2 weeks to reduce gas or stomach discomfort. This is normal and passes as the gut adjusts.

🚨 Call the Doctor If
⚠️
No improvement after 5–7 days of consistent plan
⚠️
Stomach pain is getting worse, not better
⚠️
She stops eating or has no appetite
⚠️
Any blood noticed
⚠️
Vomiting or fever alongside constipation
💗 This plan is designed for a healthy 10-year-old and provides approximately 22–28g of fiber per day when followed fully. Always follow the guidance of her doctor first.
